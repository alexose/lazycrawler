LazyCrawler.

LazyCrawler is an unusual web spider.

  • Aimless indexing

    Where most spiders try to build a complete index of a site, LazyCrawler goes where it pleases.

    • URL Favoritism
      • It prefers urls that are simple, especially with tildes.
    • Poor HTML appreciation
      • It prefers HTML filled with MS Office tags, unclosed paragraphs, and other indicators of hand-crafted content.
